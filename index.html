Why Paint Factor Matters

Our platform solves the core inefficiencies in the painting industry with powerful estimation tools and transparent project management.

AI-Enhanced Estimation

Upload photos or blueprints and our AI technology automatically calculates surface areas, identifies paintable components, and generates accurate estimates within minutes.

Atomic-Level Task Database

Access a comprehensive database of painting tasks with industry-standard production rates, helping contractors create consistently accurate bids without starting from scratch.

Dynamic Work Scope

Homeowners can customize their project scope in real-time, adding or removing items to see how it affects pricing, ensuring transparency and budget control.

Project Management Suite

Contractors can track progress, manage crews, log time, and communicate with clients all in one platform, eliminating the need for multiple disconnected tools.

Material Management

Automatically generate materials lists based on project scope, create purchase orders, and track inventory to streamline procurement and reduce waste.

Contractor Discovery

Homeowners can find reputable painters in their area, compare verified reviews, and request quotes based on their project scope, making hiring decisions data-driven.

Who Paint Factor Serves

Our platform bridges the gap between painting contractors and homeowners, solving key problems for both groups.

Painting Contractors

From solo painters to large companies, Paint Factor provides the tools to streamline operations and grow your business.

Problem

Inaccurate estimating, inefficient site visits, and disjointed project management tools.

Solution

Standardized estimation, AI-powered measurements, and an all-in-one project management system.

Homeowners

Get clarity and control over your painting project from start to finish, without the typical uncertainty.

Problem

Difficulty obtaining transparent quotes, unclear project scopes, and uncertainty about contractor reliability.

Solution

Detailed project scopes, customizable estimates, and contractor verification with aggregated reviews.

Industry Partners

Paint suppliers, manufacturers, and service providers can connect with a targeted audience of active painting professionals and customers.

Problem

Fragmented market access, inefficient ordering processes, and limited data on product performance.

Solution

Integrated marketplace, streamlined material ordering, and valuable industry insights.

Development Roadmap

Paint Factor is being built with a phased approach, focusing on delivering immediate value while building toward a comprehensive solution.

Phase 1: MVP Launch

Q2 2025

Core estimation engine with standard task rates, basic AI measurement capabilities, contractor profiles, and homeowner project creation.

Phase 2: Core Functionality

Q4 2025

Enhanced project management suite, advanced time tracking, automated material ordering, client portal, and improved mobile capabilities.

Phase 3: Advanced Features

Q2 2026

Machine learning for continuous improvement, comprehensive analytics dashboard, enterprise features, advanced supplier integrations, and mobile app launch.

Phase 4: Expansion

Q4 2026

Platform expansion to adjacent trades, advanced AI capabilities, deeper integrations, and enterprise-level features for large organizations.
